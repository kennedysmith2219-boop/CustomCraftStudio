Our Story

How Sunrise Cafe came to be — a love letter to mornings, coffee, and community.

From a Dream to Your Neighborhood Spot

Sunrise Cafe was born from a simple belief: mornings matter. Maria Gonzalez, a lifelong baker and coffee enthusiast, dreamed of creating a space where the community could gather, slow down, and start their day with something beautiful.

After years of perfecting her pastry recipes and studying coffee roasting, Maria found the perfect spot — a sun-drenched corner with big windows, warm wood floors, and the most incredible morning light. Sunrise Cafe opened its doors in 2025, and it's been a labor of love ever since.

Every item on our menu is made from scratch, using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. Our coffee beans come from small-batch roasters who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Our pastries are baked fresh each morning, filling the cafe with the irresistible aroma of butter, sugar, and warmth.

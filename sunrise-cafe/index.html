Our story

Born from a love of good coffee and community

Sunrise Cafe started with a simple idea: create a place where neighbors gather, conversations flow, and every cup of coffee is made with care. We believe in the magic of mornings — the quiet hours when the world feels full of possibility.

From our hand-picked single-origin beans to our from-scratch bakery, everything we do is rooted in quality, warmth, and community.
